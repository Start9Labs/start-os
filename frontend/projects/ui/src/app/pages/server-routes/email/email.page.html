Email Settings

EmbassyOS comes with a built-in SMTP server for sending emails. It is currently useful for two purposes:
  1. Your Embassy can send you an email when you have a new notification.
  2. Installed services can send emails for a variety of purposes, such as forgot password flows, invitations, alerts, etc.
